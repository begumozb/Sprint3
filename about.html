About Us

Nature Friendly Club;

is a community of volunteer students and academicians who strive to ensure that all living beings on campus live in a healthy and safe manner.Its main purpose is to meet the nutritional, shelter and health needs of the animals in Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University campuses and around the campuses, thus to ensure that they can continue their lives in the best way possible. In this way, it provides that people and animals live in the healthiest and most reliable way, without limiting each other's living spaces and living rights within the campus.
